Shipping Logistics

Our shipping & delivery timelines quoted are business days - Monday to Friday only, excluding weekends.
They vary depending on the delivery option chosen. Fill in our online form to place an order :)

Free delivery in SA on orders over R350!.

Postage or Collection

  • Door Delivery: Delivery time starts from the day you place your order to the day one of our delivery associates makes a first attempt to deliver to you.
  • Pick Up Station: Delivery time starts from the day you place your order to when you receive the first SMS to pick up your order from our pickup station.
    Ensure you pickup your item within 5 days, otherwise it will be cancelled.
Max delivery attempts is 3x over 5 days (7.00 am - 6.20pm )! After which the item will be cancelled.
